Introduction

Despite the challenges posed by my surroundings, I was determined to clear the JEE Mains exam and pursue my dream of becoming an engineer. This is the story of my journey, from my humble beginnings in Roh to achieving success in one of the most competitive exams in India.

Exam Experience

jee session 1 exam date- 25 january 2024
jee session 2 exam date- 6 april 2023
board exam date- 1st February to 7th February 2024

In the weeks leading up to my JEE Mains exam on January 25th, I had almost given up. My focus shifted entirely towards my board exams, which were starting on February 1st. Given the requirement of securing at least 75% in the boards, I prioritized them over JEE Mains, dedicating my time and energy to ensure I met this critical benchmark.
         For three months, I halted my JEE preparation to concentrate solely on my board studies. Despite this shift in focus, when I sat for the JEE Mains exam, I approached it with a calm mindset, without the intense pressure I had initially felt. To my surprise and delight, I managed to score an 89 percentile.
         Achieving this score, even after pausing my JEE preparation for such a significant period, was a pivotal moment for me. It instilled a newfound confidence that, with dedicated effort and a structured plan, I could indeed crack the JEE. This unexpected success reaffirmed my belief in my abilities and motivated me to continue pursuing my dream with renewed vigor.
         I resumed my preparation with renewed determination, focusing intensely on the next attempt. On April 6th, I appeared for the second attempt of JEE Mains. This time, my efforts paid off even more significantly—I scored a 95.5 percentile, qualifying for the JEE Advanced.
         Despite this achievement and the opportunity to secure a seat in an NIT, I made a strategic decision. I chose not to fill the JEE Advanced form and instead decided to take an early drop year to prepare more thoroughly. I believed that with an additional year of focused preparation, I could achieve even better results and secure a spot in one of the top IITs. This decision marked a new chapter in my journey, one driven by confidence, strategic planning, and a relentless pursuit of excellence.
         After taking the drop, I started preparing again with the help of Physics Wallah (PW). This renewed preparation was rigorous and well-structured. On February 1st, 2024, I sat for the JEE Mains exam once again and achieved a remarkable 98.16 percentile. This accomplishment was a testament to my hard work, perseverance, and the effectiveness of my preparation strategy.
